    • About
    The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, which plays in the Indian Premier League (IPL) .They were found in 2008 and they play their home games at M.A. Chidambaram stadium in chennai. They received a 2 year ban along with rajasthan royals .They are the only team to qualify for the playoffs in all the IPL seasons they have played.They have won 3 IPL titles so far
